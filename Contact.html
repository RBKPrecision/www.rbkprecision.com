02Common Questions

Answers to questions we hear most often.

— Turnaround

How quickly can you deliver?

Lead times depend on complexity, quantity, and material availability. We'll give you a firm delivery schedule with your quotation. Urgent jobs are handled case by case — contact us to discuss.

— Minimum Order

What's your minimum order quantity?

We work on everything from single prototypes and first articles to high-volume repeat production orders. There's no minimum — we assess every job on its merits.

— Materials

What materials do you work with?

Steel, stainless steel, aluminium, brass, copper, titanium, and engineering plastics including PEEK, Delrin, and Nylon. If your material isn't listed, ask us — we'll let you know if we can accommodate it.

— Quality

Do you provide inspection reports?

Yes. We provide first article inspection reports as standard. Full dimensional reports, material certifications, and material traceability documentation are available on request.
